About Edupro SMS

Who We Are

Edupro SMS is Zimbabwe's dedicated school management platform — designed from the ground up for the realities of Zimbabwean education, not adapted from a foreign system.

Zimbabwe's schools operate in a unique and demanding environment: a dual-currency economy, two major curriculum boards (ZIMSEC and Cambridge International), inconsistent internet connectivity, and limited IT support staff. Generic school management software — designed for schools in the UK, USA, or South Africa — simply does not fit.

Edupro SMS was built by Zimbabweans, for Zimbabwean schools. Every feature — from EcoCash fee collection and ZiG invoicing, to ZIMSEC O-Level grade scales and Ministry of Primary & Secondary Education reporting formats — is designed for the specific context of Zimbabwean school administration.

Our platform runs offline-first on a local school server, ensuring that load-shedding, fibre cuts, and ZESA outages never prevent teachers from marking attendance, bursars from issuing receipts, or administrators from generating report cards. Schools that want cloud backup and remote access can add our ZimHPC integration — backed by the University of Zimbabwe's High Performance Computing Centre, keeping all student data on Zimbabwean soil.

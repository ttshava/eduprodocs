Platform Overview

The Edupro SMS Platform

Edupro SMS is a modular, offline-capable school management platform designed specifically for Zimbabwean primary and secondary schools. Schools activate only the modules they need — starting from a single module and expanding as their capacity grows.
SIM-100
Student Information Management
The central registry for every learner — ECD through Form 6

SIM-100 is the foundation of Edupro SMS. Every other module draws its student data from this central registry. It replaces paper registers and scattered spreadsheets with one structured, searchable database.

Full student demographics (name, DOB, national ID, gender, address, nationality, home language)
Parent/guardian profiles with multiple contact numbers
Medical records: allergies, chronic conditions, vaccinations
Academic history: previous schools, transfer records, O-Level results
Photo ID capture and student ID card printing
ZIMSEC registration number storage
Ministry of Primary & Secondary Education reporting export
Bulk import from Excel / existing school registers
Offline CapableZIMSEC ReadyCambridge ReadyECD – Form 6
ADM-200
Admissions & Enrolment
Digital application processing from enquiry to class placement

ADM-200 digitises the entire admissions process — from online application forms and document collection through entrance test results to final class placement and registration fee collection.

Online application portal for parents (accessible from any phone)
Document checklist: birth certificate, immunisation card, O-Level results
Entrance test scheduling and result recording
Waiting list management with priority rules
Class placement with capacity enforcement
Acceptance letter and offer generation (PDF)
Registration fee capture linked to FIN-500
Automatic SIM-100 profile creation on enrolment
Online ApplicationsWaiting ListsPDF Letters

Course creation for every ZIMSEC and Cambridge subject
Video lessons, PDFs, quizzes, and assignments — all offline
Automated grade sync to RPT-800 report cards
Discussion forums for student-teacher interaction
Teacher gradebook with ZIMSEC/Cambridge grade mapping
Student progress dashboards and completion tracking
Mobile-responsive (works on smartphones & tablets)

ATT-300
Attendance Management
From daily registers to Ministry attendance reports — automated

ATT-300 replaces paper attendance registers with a fast digital system. Teachers mark attendance on any device in under 2 minutes per class. Attendance data flows automatically to report cards and parent notifications.

Morning roll call on any browser or tablet
Period-by-period attendance for secondary schools
Automatic SMS/WhatsApp to parents on absence (via COM-400)
Late arrival and early departure logging
Attendance % per student per term — shown on report cards
ZIMSEC 80% attendance threshold alerts
Class and school-wide attendance analytics
Ministry R&C form export (attendance summary)

COM-400
Communications Portal
SMS, WhatsApp, and email to every parent — from one screen

COM-400 connects the school to parents through Zimbabwe's most-used channels — WhatsApp, SMS, and email — directly from Edupro SMS. No need for separate messaging apps or manual copying of numbers.

Bulk SMS to all parents, a single class, or individual students
WhatsApp message broadcasting (requires WhatsApp Business API)
Automated fee reminder messages from FIN-500
Absence notifications triggered by ATT-300
Report card and invoice delivery via email (PDF attachment)
School announcements: term dates, events, closures
Message delivery reports and read receipts
Parent opt-out management (POTRAZ compliance)

FIN-500
School Fees Management
USD & ZiG · EcoCash · Innbucks · RTGS · Full audit trail

FIN-500 is the complete bursary system for Zimbabwe schools — handling multi-currency invoicing, all local payment methods, real-time debtor dashboards, and financial reports for school boards.

Per-grade, per-term fee structure with multiple levy types
EcoCash, Innbucks, RTGS, USD cash, ZiG cash, bank transfer
Steward Bank auto-reconciliation integration
Dual-currency invoicing (USD and ZiG with configurable exchange rate)
QR-code receipts with parent verification portal
Real-time debtor dashboard by class, grade, and school
Sibling discounts and scholarship/bursary management
Board-ready reports: arrears ageing, collection rate, bad debt
EcoCash ReadyUSD & ZiGInnbucksRTGSQR Receipts

Academic Reporting System
ZIMSEC & Cambridge report cards — generated in bulk in minutes

RPT-800 eliminates manual report card typing, calculation errors, and unsigned reports going home — by generating professional PDF report cards for every student in one click.

Cambridge IGCSE and AS/A-Level grading
Primary school competency-based reports (ECD – Grade 7)
Automatic class position, aggregate, and averages
Bulk PDF export — entire school in one ZIP file
Email report cards to parents directly from the system
Teacher remarks and Head of School comments
Attendance % from ATT-300 auto-populates each report card

Asset Management
Know exactly what your school owns, where it is, and what it's worth

Asset register: equipment, furniture, ICT, vehicles, buildings
Purchase date, cost, supplier, and warranty records
Asset location tracking (block, classroom, lab)
Condition ratings: Good / Fair / Needs Repair / Condemned
Depreciation calculation (straight-line and reducing balance)
Maintenance schedule and service history log
Asset disposal and write-off recording
Board-ready asset valuation report (USD and ZiG)

On-site and remote staff training for full system adoption

TRN-1000 is not a software module — it is the structured training programme delivered by Edupro SMS specialists to ensure every member of school staff can use the system effectively from day one.

On-site full-day training for administrators and bursars
Teacher training: mark entry, Moodle course creation, attendance
IT administrator training: server management and backups
Head of School / Board reporting walkthrough
Video training library on Moodle (accessible offline)
Printed quick-reference cards for each staff role
Remote support via WhatsApp and screen sharing
Annual refresher sessions for new staff

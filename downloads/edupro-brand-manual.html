Brand Foundation
01 — Brand Foundation
Who We Are
Edupro SMS is Zimbabwe's dedicated school management platform — built for the realities of Zimbabwean education: dual-currency payments, ZIMSEC and Cambridge curricula, unreliable internet, and schools that deserve technology as capable as their teachers.

Brand Mission

To make every Zimbabwean school — from a rural primary in Binga to an elite secondary in Borrowdale — run with the efficiency, transparency, and professionalism it deserves through technology built specifically for Zimbabwe.

Brand Personality

  • Confident: We know Zimbabwe's schools. We built this for them.
  • Trustworthy: Schools trust us with their most sensitive data — student records, finances, staff.
  • Accessible: We speak plainly. No jargon. No complicated tech language.
  • Local: USD and ZiG. EcoCash and Innbucks. ZIMSEC and Cambridge. Always Zimbabwean.
  • Progressive: We move Zimbabwean education forward, one school at a time.

Brand Voice

  • Speak directly to the Headmaster, Bursar, and IT administrator — not to a generic global audience.
  • Use active voice. Short sentences. No fluff.
  • Reference Zimbabwe specifically: provinces, payment methods, curriculum boards, school types.
  • Positive and empowering — never patronising.
  • Professional but warm. We are a partner, not a vendor.

Colour Usage Rules

✓ Do
Use Edupro Red (#FF0527) for primary call-to-action buttons, key headlines, and brand accents.

Use Edupro Dark (#1a0a0e) for dark backgrounds and primary navigation.

White text on Red or Dark backgrounds always.
✗ Don't
Never use red text on a red background.

Never use dark text (#1a0a0e) on Edupro Red — contrast is insufficient.

Never use approximate reds (orange-red, pink-red). Always use the exact hex value.

Never use red for error states — use standard semantic red (#dc2626).

Logo Usage
04 — Logo
Logo Usage Guidelines
The Edupro SMS logo is the single most recognisable element of our brand. Consistent, correct usage builds trust. Never alter, distort, or recolour the logo.

Logo on Different Backgrounds

Minimum Clear Space

Always maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the letter "E" in "Edupro". No other graphic elements, text, or photos should appear within this zone.

Minimum Size

  • Print: No smaller than 25mm wide
  • Digital: No smaller than 100px wide
  • Social media profile picture: Use the icon/monogram version at small sizes
✓ Do
Scale proportionally.
Use on approved backgrounds only.
Use the correct version (light/dark) for the background.
Request the original PNG or SVG file from the design team.
✗ Don't
Stretch or squash the logo.
Change the logo colours.
Add drop shadows or effects.
Place on a busy photograph without a solid colour overlay.
Recreate the logo in another font.

Design Standards
05 — Design Standards
Digital & Print Standards
Consistent design across all materials — website, documents, social media, and print — reinforces brand trust. Follow these standards for all Edupro SMS communications.

Digital Design (Web & App)

  • Primary button: Background #FF0527 · White text · Border-radius 8px · Padding 12px 24px · Font-weight 700
  • Secondary button: Transparent background · #FF0527 border 2px · #FF0527 text · Same radius/padding
  • Card shadows: box-shadow: 0 4px 24px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)
  • Border radius: Cards 12–16px · Inputs 8px · Tags/badges 6px
  • Icon style: Line icons only (2px stroke) — consistent with Heroicons / Feather Icons style
  • Spacing unit: 8px base grid (8, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64px)

Print Materials

  • Document size: A4 (210 × 297mm) for all formal documents
  • Safe margin: 20mm all sides for print-critical content
  • Bleed: 3mm bleed on all edges for professionally printed materials
  • Resolution: 300 DPI minimum for print; 150 DPI minimum for large-format banners
  • Colour mode: CMYK for print files; RGB/Hex for digital
  • Paper stock: 120gsm for letterheads; 250gsm for business cards; 170gsm for brochures

Social Media Dimensions

  • Instagram / LinkedIn post: 1080 × 1080px (square) or 1080 × 1350px (portrait)
  • Instagram / LinkedIn cover: 1584 × 396px
  • Facebook cover: 820 × 312px
  • Twitter/X header: 1500 × 500px
  • WhatsApp profile: 500 × 500px
  • YouTube thumbnail: 1280 × 720px

Get Started

How to Get Started

The process from first contact to go-live typically takes 2–4 weeks, depending on school readiness.
1
Contact our sales team
Call, WhatsApp, or email to schedule a free 30-minute demonstration of the full system. We'll show you live report cards, fee collection, attendance, and the Moodle LMS for your school's specific context.
2
Receive your formal quotation
We issue a formal quotation in your school's name covering the setup fee, chosen subscription plan, and any optional extras. Suitable for Ministry procurement and LPO approval processes.
3
Pay setup fee & schedule installation
Once the setup fee is confirmed, our technical team contacts you to schedule the on-site installation. We procure any required hardware and coordinate with your IT staff or caretaker.
4
Installation & staff training
Our technicians install and configure everything on-site. A full training day covers all roles: Head of School, Bursar, teachers, and the IT administrator. Your school leaves ready to operate independently.
5
Go live — supported from day one
Your school is live on Edupro SMS. Our support team is available via WhatsApp and email throughout the first term. Most schools are fully self-sufficient within 4–6 weeks of go-live.

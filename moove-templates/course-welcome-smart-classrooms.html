Designed for School Headmasters & Deputy Heads

This is Edupro's flagship leadership programme for school leaders ready to drive digital transformation in their institutions.

Digital transformation is reshaping education. Today's school leaders must understand how to create modern learning environments that prepare learners for the future.

This course provides practical guidance on planning, implementing, managing, and sustaining Smart Classrooms using appropriate technologies and proven leadership strategies.

Throughout the course you will collaborate with fellow school leaders, exchange ideas, and develop a Smart Classroom Implementation Plan tailored to your school.

🌐 Offline Moodle LMS setup & management 📱 Tablet management with Headwind MDM
📊 Learning analytics & reporting 🔒 CCTV, power & physical security
🖌 School Implementation Plan development 🏆 Certificate of completion

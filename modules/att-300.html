ATT-300

Attendance Management

Accurate attendance records are a Ministry requirement and a safeguarding essential. ATT-300 replaces paper roll-call books with a fast digital register that works fully offline and automatically alerts parents when their child is absent.

Daily Session Register

Zimbabwean primary and secondary schools track attendance at two points per day: morning assembly/period 1 and afternoon return. ATT-300 mirrors this structure digitally.

Morning Session Register

Class teachers mark morning attendance from their classroom computer or tablet during period 1. Students are marked Present, Absent, or Late. The register locks automatically after a configurable cut-off time (e.g., 08:30).

Afternoon Session Register

A second register at the start of the afternoon session captures learners who left during the break or arrived late after lunch. This is critical for boarding schools managing learner movements.

Absence Reason Capture

When a parent notifies the school of an absence (phone call, WhatsApp, or note), the registrar or class teacher updates the absence reason: Sick, Family Emergency, Suspension, Authorised Absence, or Unknown. This affects reporting.

Whole-School Morning View

The Deputy Headmaster's dashboard shows a real-time whole-school attendance summary: total present, total absent by grade, and a list of unreported absences requiring follow-up — all updated as teachers submit their registers.

Staff Attendance & Lesson Coverage

Teacher absenteeism directly affects learner outcomes. ATT-300 tracks staff attendance and lesson coverage to give management full visibility.

Staff Daily Register

  • Each staff member signs in digitally at the start of the day
  • Late arrivals are flagged with actual arrival time
  • Absent staff are automatically identified when periods start without coverage
  • Leave types: Sick Leave, Annual Leave, Study Leave, INSET, Official Duty
  • Supply/substitute teacher assignments recorded

Lesson Coverage Tracking

  • Period-by-period lesson completion log per class
  • Covered lessons recorded with covering teacher name
  • Uncovered periods flagged for head of department follow-up
  • Monthly coverage reports per subject and teacher
  • Curriculum pacing: lessons taught vs lessons planned

Absenteeism Trends & Analytics

ATT-300 transforms daily register data into management intelligence — helping school leaders identify patterns before they become problems.

Student Absenteeism Reports

View absenteeism rates by individual student, class, grade, or whole school over any date range. Identify learners with more than 5% absenteeism for welfare follow-up.

Day-of-Week Patterns

Identify if absenteeism spikes on Mondays (post-weekend) or Fridays (early departures). Use this data to schedule important lessons strategically and address pattern truancy.

At-Risk Learner Flagging

The system automatically flags students with three or more consecutive unexplained absences for senior management review, supporting early intervention before school dropout occurs.

Ministry Compliance Reporting

ATT-300 generates all attendance reports required by the Ministry of Primary and Secondary Education for provincial and district school inspector visits.

1

Monthly Attendance Returns

Generate monthly attendance summaries per class in the Ministry's standard format. Reports show total school days, present days, absent days, and percentage attendance for each learner.

2

Term Attendance Certificates

Print or PDF individual attendance certificates per student per term — required by ZIMSEC for examination candidates who must demonstrate minimum attendance to qualify for external examinations.

3

Inspector-Ready Class Registers

Produce a full class register showing day-by-day attendance for any class for any period. Formatted for A3 printing with the class teacher's signature block — exactly what school inspectors request.

4

Whole-School Statistical Returns

End-of-term statistical attendance tables by gender, grade, and school level — ready for submission to the District Schools Inspector as part of the school's termly returns.

Offline-First Design

Attendance cannot wait for the internet. ATT-300 runs entirely on the school's local server — no connection to ZimHPC cloud is needed for daily operations.

Local Server Operation

  • All register marking happens on the school's local network
  • No internet connection required to open, complete, or submit registers
  • Data stored on school's local server (on-premise)
  • Multiple teachers can submit simultaneously over the school LAN
  • No data loss if server loses power mid-session (auto-save every 30 seconds)

Sync with ZimHPC Cloud

  • When internet is available, attendance data syncs to ZimHPC cloud
  • Enables parent SMS/WhatsApp absenteeism alerts (requires brief connectivity)
  • Cloud backup protects data against local server failure
  • Multi-school dashboard for proprietors with multiple school campuses
  • Sync is automatic — no staff action required

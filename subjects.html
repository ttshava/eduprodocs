One system — both curricula. Edupro SMS manages ZIMSEC and Cambridge streams within the same installation. Students are tagged to their curriculum pathway, and the correct subject set, grading scale, and report card format are applied automatically. Schools running dual-curriculum programmes — common in Harare private schools — can manage everything from a single dashboard. Subject registers are updated annually in line with current ZIMSEC and Cambridge International syllabi.
